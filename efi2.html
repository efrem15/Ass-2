#1:My biography

I am Efrem born to my father Tesfai Ytbarek and my mother Elsa Asgodom. Born and raised in Eritrea, I attended elementary and middle school in Areza , a town on the southern part of the country . I completed high school in 2004 from st. George secondary school and joined Eritrean institute of technology to attend a premedical school. I graduated from Orotta school of medicine in 2013.

I started my first work in Adi keyh hospital as a general practitioner .
